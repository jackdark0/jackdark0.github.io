Our modern facility is located in a peaceful wooded setting where we employ state of the art dental equipment such as digital x-rays to provide superior diagnostic images while reducing the radiation exposure dramatically and using no toxic processing chemicals. In addition to digital x-rays, our KAVO 3D PRO is a cone beam CT scanner. This imaging system is used to precisely evaluate, diagnose, and plan dental implant treatment. It can also take 2-D panoramic images & extra-oral BWX for those that struggle with the intra-oral x-ray sensors.

Our office is pleased to use the latest Trios 3shape intraoral scanners for digital impressions. This technology eliminates the messy and claustrophobic traditional impression for many procedures and replaces it with a digital 3-D scan. This is truly a revolution in dentistry.

Michael Leonard, D.D.S.

Dr. Michael Leonard is a 1995 graduate of The University of Detroit Mercy School of Dentistry and completed a General Practice Residency at the Veterans Administration Hospital of Ann Arbor/University of Michigan Hospital in 1996. He has been practicing in the Kalamazoo area since July 1996.

Advanced training allows Dr. Leonard to provide Intravenous Conscious Sedation to patients that prefer or require sedation to complete their dental care. Dr. Leonard has also achieved Mastership in the college of Sedation in Dentistry within the American Dental Society of Anesthesiology.
